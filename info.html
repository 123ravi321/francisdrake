Who was Francis Drake and what was his life like?

Francis Drake, born in around 1540, was the oldest of twelve sons of Edmund Drake.
He was an English explorer and privateer.

Where was Francis Drake From and where did he go?

He was from England.
His 1577 Voyage was to South America.

What was Francis Drake known for?

Circumnavigating the world.
This was the first English circumnavigation and the third overall.
